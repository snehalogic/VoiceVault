How it works

Three steps from audio
to verdict.

VoiceVault keeps the pipeline transparent so you always know why a clip is flagged.

Step 01
Upload audio
Drop any WAV, MP3, or recorded clip. The frontend strips it to raw PCM and streams it to the FastAPI backend in chunks ready for inference.
Step 02
RawNet2 analyses in 3-second chunks
Each chunk is fed to a PyTorch RawNet2 model trained on ASVspoof 2019. The model processes raw waveforms — no hand-crafted features, no spectrograms.
Step 03
Verdict + confidence heatmap
You get a per-chunk confidence score and a colour-coded heatmap showing exactly where the model detected synthetic artefacts.
